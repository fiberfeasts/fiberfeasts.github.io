Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 24, 2026

Welcome to FiberFeasts! Your privacy is incredibly important to us. Because we believe in keeping our recipes simple, wholesome, and completely safe for cooking enthusiasts of all ages, our privacy policy is straightforward and easy to understand.

1. What Information We Collect

Absolutely nothing! We do not offer user registration, we do not require you to sign up, and we do not collect your name, email address, or any other personal details just to browse our delicious high-fiber recipes.

2. Cookies & Tracking

  • No Tracking Cookies: We do not use third-party advertising trackers, social media analytics pixels, or cookie monitors that collect your behavior across other sites.
  • Theme Preferences: If you toggle Dark/Light Mode, your browser might remember that preference using a small built-in storage feature called Local Storage. This information stays entirely on your own device and is never sent to our servers.

3. Web Hosting Platform

Our website is hosted using GitHub Pages. Like almost all web hosting platforms on the internet, GitHub's servers may automatically record simple logs (such as your device's IP address) for basic security purposes to keep the website running securely. This information is handled exclusively by GitHub under their own standard safety protocols.

4. Links to Other Sites

Our interactive recipe sheets might occasionally link to other fun cooking websites or external educational articles. Once you click a link and leave FiberFeasts, we don't control those external sites, so we recommend checking out their privacy practices if you are curious!

5. Getting in Touch

If you choose to write to us with recipe submissions or general culinary questions, we will only use your return email address to reply directly to you. We will never sell, lease, or use your email address for commercial advertising lists.
