Peanut Butter Banana Chia Pudding

A creamy, high-fiber breakfast that tastes almost like dessert. Chia seeds absorb liquid to create a thick pudding while packing a massive amount of fiber into every bite!

🍽️ Serves: 1-2
⏱️ Prep Time: 10 mins
❄️ Chill Time: 2 hrs

🛒 Ingredients

  • ¼ cup chia seeds
  • 1 cup milk (regular dairy, almond, or oat)
  • 2 tbsp creamy peanut butter
  • 1 tbsp honey or maple syrup (optional)
  • ½ tsp vanilla extract (optional)
  • 1 medium banana

Let's Get Cooking!

1

Cut the banana in half. Mash one half with a fork in a small bowl until it is mostly smooth. Save the other half for later.

2

In a jar or a medium bowl, combine the mashed banana, milk, peanut butter, honey, and vanilla extract. Whisk everything together until the peanut butter is mostly mixed in (a few small lumps are totally fine).

3

Pour the chia seeds into the wet mixture and whisk vigorously to make sure all the seeds are submerged.

4

Let the mixture sit on the counter for exactly 5 minutes, then give it one more good stir. This stops the chia seeds from clumping together at the bottom!

5

Cover the jar or bowl with a lid or plastic wrap.

6

Place it in the fridge for at least 2 hours, or ideally overnight. The chia seeds will expand and turn the liquid into a thick pudding.

7

When you are ready to eat, slice up the remaining half of the banana and put the slices on top. Dig in!

💡

Fiber Fact!

Just two tablespoons of chia seeds contain almost 10 grams of fiber! Combined with the banana, this breakfast will keep you full and energized all morning.
